How to Add Your Own YouTube Songs

1

Find YouTube Videos

Go to YouTube and find birthday songs or celebration videos you want to add to Prachu's playlist.

2

Get YouTube Embed Link

Click "Share" on the YouTube video, then "Embed". Copy the URL that looks like: https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID

3

Update the Playlist

Add a new song item in the HTML with the data-youtube attribute containing your YouTube embed URL.
